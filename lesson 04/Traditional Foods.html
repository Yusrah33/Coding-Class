1 Masal Dosa Masala Dosa is a traditional South Indian delicacy that combines taste, texture, and aroma in a unique way. It consists of a thin, golden-brown crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram lentils), giving it a slightly tangy flavor and a light, crispy texture.The dosa is typically filled with a savory potato masala—boiled potatoes mashed and sautéed with mustard seeds, onions, green chilies, curry leaves, turmeric, and sometimes ginger or garlic. This filling is tucked inside the dosa like a wrap or served in the center.Masala Dosa is usually accompanied by sides like sambar (a spicy lentil and vegetable stew) and a variety of chutneys, including coconut, tomato, and mint. It’s commonly enjoyed as a breakfast or brunch dish but is loved at any time of the day.
2 Sadya Sadya is a traditional vegetarian feast from the South Indian state of Kerala, typically served on a fresh banana leaf during festivals, weddings, and special occasions like Onam.A classic Sadya can include 20 to over 30 dishes, all carefully prepared to balance the six basic tastes: sweet, sour, salty, bitter, pungent, and astringent. Some common items include:
  • Rice
  • Dal
  • Sambar
  • Rasam
  • Avial
  • Thoran
  • Pickles and Banana Chips
  • Payasam
3 Karimeen Pollichathu Karimeen Pollichathu is a signature delicacy from the cuisine of Kerala. It features Karimeen, also known as Pearl Spot fish, a freshwater fish native to Kerala's lakes and lagoons. The dish involves marinating the whole fish in a flavorful blend of spices—typically including chili powder, turmeric, black pepper, garlic, and lemon juice—then it's gently pan-fried to seal in the flavors. After that, the fish is smothered in a spicy, tangy masala made with onions, tomatoes, curry leaves, and coconut oil, then wrapped in a banana leaf.The wrapped fish is either grilled, steamed, or pan-roasted, allowing the banana leaf to infuse a distinct, earthy aroma into the dish. The result is moist, flaky fish with a spicy outer layer and a smoky, aromatic essence from the leaf.
