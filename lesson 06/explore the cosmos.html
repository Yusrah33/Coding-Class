Planets of Our Solar System

  1. Mercury: The smallest and fastest planet, zooming around the sun in only 88 days.
  2. Venus: Venus' thick, toxic atmosphere traps heat in a runaway 'greenhouse effect'.
  3. Earth: The only planet known to have an atmosphere containing free oxygen, oceans of water on its surface, and life.
  4. Mars: Mars is home to the tallest mountain in the solar system, Olympus Mons, and the deepest, longest valley, Valles Marineris.
  5. Jupiter: The largest planet, with a massive magnetic field and more than 79 moons.
  6. Saturn: Known for it stunning rings,Saturn has numerous moons including Titan, which has its own atmosphere.
  7. Uranus: An ice giant with a unique sideways rotation and a pale blue color due to methane in the atmosphere.
  8. Neptune: Neptune is the windiest planet, with supersonic winds and a dark blue color due to its atmospheric composition.
