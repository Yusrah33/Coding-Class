Bolster Girls Campus

Bolster Girls Campus is a residential school for girls located in Kerala, India. It offers a high-quality education that blends cultural and traditional values with modern academic excellence. The campus provides specialized coaching for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and CUET, along with support for international university admissions.With personalized education plans, academic competency assessments, and career guidance programs,Bolster Girls Campus aims to empower students to achieve their academic and personal aspirations. It also emphasizes holistic development, fostering critical thinking, self-understanding, and a strong sense of identity.

Academic Curriculum

  • Offers Science stream based on Kerala Syllabus(SCERT)
  • Rigorous preparation for exams like NEET, JEE, and CUET is integrated into the curriculum.
  • Tailored learning initiatives address each student's unique strengths and aspirations, offering customized academic pathways and mentorship.
  • Assistance is provided for international university admissions, empowering students to pursue education abroad.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Sports and Physical Fitness

    Opportunities for students to engage in sports and maintain physical well-being.

  • Cultural Programs

    Events and activities that celebrate traditions and foster creativity, such as music, dance, and drama

  • Clubs

    Platforms for students to explore interests like science, literature, or environmental conservation.

  • Skill Development Workshops

    Sessions on public speaking, leadership, and other essential life skills.

Student and Teacher Community

The campus is home to a diverse group of students who are encouraged to excel academically and participate in extracurricular activities. The environment promotes collaboration, inclusivity, and mutual respect among peers. Faculty consists of experienced and dedicated educators who provide personalized mentorship and guidance. They are committed to nurturing each student's potential and preparing them for competitive exams and future opportunities.
